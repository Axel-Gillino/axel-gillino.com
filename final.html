AXEL GILLINO

Designer & Interactive Art Director

His passion for flawlessly executed and engaging design is bolstered by his obsession with pixel perfection and usability. He have a hunger for the latest innovations, and continuously use what he learn to push the boundaries of technology and optimize user experience.

Not only does he use his multi-faceted design skills to execute beautiful and visionary work that solves problems, he also create new business opportunities and cultural innovation. He have a thirst for inspiration that simply cannot be quenched.
