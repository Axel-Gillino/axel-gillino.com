AXEL GILLINO

Designer & Interactive Art Director

Axel Gillino is a French multi-disciplinary designer, multimedia engineering and design graduate with over 6 years in the industry, passionate about ideas, experience and communication through good design.

His passion for flawlessly executed and engaging design is bolstered by his obsession with pixel perfection and usability. He has a hunger for the latest innovations, and continuously uses what he learns to push the boundaries of technology and optimize user experience.

Not only he uses his multi-faceted design skills to execute beautiful and visionary work that solves problems but he also creates new business opportunities and cultural innovation. He has a thirst for inspiration that simply cannot be quenched.
